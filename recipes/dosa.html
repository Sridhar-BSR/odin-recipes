dosa

Description

dosa or dosay is a thin, savoury crepe in Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black gram and rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad Dal
  • Fenugreek Seeds
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Ghee or oil

Steps

  1. Prepare Dosa Batter:Soak rice and urad dal in separate bowls for 4-6 hours.
  2. Process rice and urad dal mixture in commercial grinder or food processor.
  3. Cover mixture and let sit overnight.
  4. Prepare Masala Dosa Filling: Add oil or ghee to a skillet.
