Lasagna

Description

lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish featuring layers of flat, wide noodles, rich meat sauce (like ragu), creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • ground beef or sausage
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta or cottage cheese
  • mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • lasagna noodles
  • herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400ºF.
  2. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce.
  3. Continue layering the lasagna.
  4. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and finish baking.
